Use of the App

The app is built to work both online and offline, but an active internet connection will optimize the app’s performance with more accuracy. Qiblah Direction and Tasbih require no active internet connection but Salaah time at a specific location/ region, Hijri calendar and Find Mosque Nearby requires an active internet connection to synchronize with the GMT, Moon and Google Maps.
